Berislav Bracun


Berislav Bracun, one of HMM operators and renowned amateur astronomer had unexpectedly left us. He was well known in the local astronomical comunity as a tireless popularizator of astronomy, amateur telescope maker, friend and collegue. We hope he joined the stars he loved so much. We all miss him a lot!
